Our Story



In the Summer of 2016, Boulevard Salad was born as the ideal path in life for founder Jeffrey Laudenslager. Being that we were in Los Angeles - home of the Dodgers, the Lakers, breathtaking coasts, and some of the most incredible food in the nation - it only made sense that we should spread the love and deliciousness of Boulevard Salad through a food truck. All our foods are named after some of the most exciting and unique boulevards in Los Angeles, and we take pride in knowing that our food is not only healthy and delicious, but also that we use only the finest ingredients. We look forward to spreading the Boulevard Salad love throughout Los Angeles in 2016 and beyond!



Jeffrey Laudenslager, founder of SumoSaladTruck, began in the food industry as a server for eight years before he began his journey with Boulevard Salad. Growing up in Pennsylvania, Jeffrey worked hard to make his dream of owning his own business in the food industry into a reality. At twenty three years old, he left his home for Vegas to realize his goal and worked as a waiter in the Strip to gain experience. Soon, he moved to Los Angeles in order to start a new chapter of his life and begin preparing for a new career path in the food industry.
